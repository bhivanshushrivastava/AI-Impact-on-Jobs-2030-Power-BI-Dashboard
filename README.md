# AI Impact on Jobs 2030 Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

The AI Impact on Jobs 2030 Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report that analyzes how Artificial Intelligence is affecting industries, job roles, salaries, automation, and future workforce demand. The dashboard provides insights into AI risk, job growth, and upskilling needs across different sectors.

## Objective

* Analyze AI risk across industries.
* Identify future job demand trends.
* Understand workforce upskilling requirements.
* Evaluate salary and job growth patterns.
* Create interactive business insights using Power BI.

## Dataset Information

The dataset includes:

* Employee ID
* Job Title
* Industry
* Country
* Education Level
* Salary
* AI Risk Score
* Future Demand Score
* Automation Level
* Upskilling Needed

## Dashboard Features

### KPIs

* Total Employees: 299
* High Risk Employees: 91
* Average AI Risk: 51.18%
* Average Future Demand: 61.32%
* Upskilling Employees: 152
* Average Salary: $122.36K

### Visualizations

* KPI Cards
* AI Risk by Industry
* Future Demand Analysis
* Job Growth by Industry
* Company Size vs AI Adoption
* Upskilling Requirement Analysis

## Key Insights

* Over 50% of employees require upskilling.
* Technology and Government sectors show higher AI risk.
* Future job demand remains strong across several industries.
* Enterprise companies have the highest AI adoption.
* AI is transforming jobs rather than replacing them completely.

## Tools & Technologies Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
  

## Project Summary

This dashboard analyzes the impact of AI on jobs, automation, and workforce demand. Using Power BI, Power Query, and DAX, the project transforms raw data into actionable insights that help understand future employment trends and workforce readiness.
